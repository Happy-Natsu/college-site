JNTUA College of Engineering

Sir Mokshagundam Vishveshwariah Road, Ananthapuramu, Andhra Pradesh-515002, INDIA

We hope all of you are already aware that JNTUA College of Engineering, Ananthapuramu is celebrating 75 Years of its wonderful journey during 16 th to 18 th of December,2021.

It is proposed to construct Platinum Jubilee Hostel with 100 rooms through the financial contribution of Alumni commemorating 75 years celebrations of JNTUA college of Engineering Anantapur. It is estimated that the cost of each hostel room works out to be Rs. 9.1 lacs. The alumni willing to contribute may donate room or rooms individually or as a batch.
In this connection all the alumni are requested to specifically mention, if they are contributing for the hostel construction of rooms in the given option form because the same account is used for general contributions also. The support to the Alumni will be highly appreciated and room/rooms will be named after the individuals or batches who make contributions.

The college here by appeals to its alumni family of different batches to contribute
liberally towards these construction works.

The prestigious Technological Institution, JNTUA College of Engineering, Anantapuram is celebrating 75 years of glorious existence on 16th, 17th and 18th of December, 2021. This temple of higher learning was established in 1946. Many illustrious alumni have walked through the portals of this great institution, bringing name and fame to their alma mater.
In this regard all alumni and esteemed people can Contribute and become a part of this 75 years celebration of JNTU ENGG COLLEGE ANANTAPUR
